📐 Technical Architecture: Multi-Agent PPO for HVAC

This demo implements a Centralized Training, Decentralized Execution (CTDE) paradigm for multi-zone building energy management. Each zone is controlled by an independent actor agent, while a shared centralized critic evaluates the global building state for stable value estimation.

🌍 Environment Dynamics

4-zone building with coupled thermal dynamics. Zones exchange heat through walls (conductance matrix). External disturbances include outdoor temperature (day/night cycle), solar radiation, and occupancy schedules.

Zone state: [temp, humidity, occupancy] Coupling: conductance matrix (4×4) Disturbance: outdoor T, solar, occupancy

🤖 MAPPO Algorithm

Per-zone Beta-distribution actors output continuous HVAC actions. Centralized critic sees full 15-dim building state. GAE for advantage estimation, PPO clipped objective with entropy bonus.

Actor: π(a_i|o_i) ~ Beta(α, β) Critic: V(s_full) → scalar Clip: ε=0.2, γ=0.99, λ=0.95

🎯 Multi-Objective Reward

Comfort (temp + humidity) weighted by occupancy, energy cost penalty, and hard budget constraint. Server room prioritizes cooling, Lab prioritizes humidity control.

R = Σ comfort_i × occ_i - 0.1 × energy_cost - 1.0 × budget_violation
